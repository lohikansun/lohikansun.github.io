Technology

  • LIDAR
    Laser Illuminating Detection and Ranging. Used to build 3D maps allowing cars to "see"
  • RADAR
    RAdar Dection and Ranging. Used to compensate for LIDAR's lack of speed monitoring. Bumber radars are used to signal the brakes when the car approaches and object
  • High-Powered Cameras
    Exterior mounted cameras give the car an overlapping view of its surroundings. Each camera has a 50 degree field of view and can see up to 30 meters
  • SONAR
    SOund Navigation and Ranging. Provides rdundant short range data, allowing the car to cross-reference and validate data from its other systems
  • GPS
    Global Positioning System. Allows the car to track its course and plot an appropriate route. Works with the on-board cameras to process the world around it, to determine how to react to any situation
