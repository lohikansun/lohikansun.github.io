Technology

  • LIDAR (Laser Illuminating Detection and Ranging)
    Used to build 3D maps allowing cars to "see"
  • RADAR (RAdar Dection and Ranging)
    Used to compensate for LIDAR's lack of speed monitoring. Bumber radars are used to signal the brakes when the car approaches and object
  • High-Powered Cameras
    Exterior mounted cameras give the car an overlapping view of its surroundings. Each camera has a 50 degree field of view and can see up to 30 meters
  • SONAR (SOund Navigation and Ranging)
    Provides rdundant short range data, allowing the car to cross-reference and validate data from its other systems
  • GPS (Global Positioning System.)
    Allows the car to track its course and plot an appropriate route. Works with the on-board cameras to process the world around it, to determine how to react to any situation

Benefits

Increased Safety

  • Self-driving cars have better reaction times than human drivers
  • No blind spots - self-driving cars have 360 degree vision, and can sense potential dangers in all directions at once
  • After several million miles of driving, the Google car has not yet been at fault in any accidents
Source: http://slidepapers.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Selfm-Driving-Cars.pdf

Efficiency

  • Self-driving cars will change city layouts
    • Parking can be moved to city outskirts, as cars can park themselves, allowing for more useful allocation of prime real estate
  • Traffic congenstion will decrease
    • Cars will be able to form intelligent traffic patterns with each other
    • Car interiors can be redesigned for comfort, so people will be willing to accept rerouting that will benefit traffic patterns
Source: R. Zakharenko, "Self-driving cars will change cities," Regional Science and Urban Economics, vol. 61, pp. 26–37

Environmental

  • Self driving cars will likely decrease car ownership, leading to a decrease in total cars, and subsequently carbon emissions
  • Sophisticated software can operate vehicles in an energy efficient manner, consuming significantly less gas than would a human-driven car
Source: http://slidepapers.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Selfm-Driving-Cars.pdf
